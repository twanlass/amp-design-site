Interview Process

We know interviewing for a new design role can be a lot. We want to make it transparent and as stress-free as possible. You'll have a chance to meet a diverse group of people from the team including PMs, Engineers, and Researchers.

Here's what to expect:

Intro

A quick intro chat with the hiring manager and time for your questions.

Case Study

We'll ask you to present one or two projects and discuss your work with the team.

Design Jam

A live collaborative design exercise with a Designer and PM from the team.

Research Partnership

A short chat about working with Research at Amplitude.

Engineering Partnership

A short chat about collaborating with Engineering at Amplitude.

Outro

A chance for final questions and wrap up with the hiring manager.

Offer

Before the offer is made, we'll ask for 3 references. We prefer at least one of those references is a previous manager.
