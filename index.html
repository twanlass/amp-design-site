Interview Process

We know interviewing for a new design role can be a lot. We want to make it transparent and as stress-free as possible. You'll have a chance to meet a diverse group of people from the team including PMs, Engineers, and Researchers. Here's what to expect:

1

Intro

Quick intro chat with the hiring manager and time for questions.

2

Case Study

Present one or two projects and discuss your work with the team.

3

Design and PM Jam

Live collaborative design exercise with a Designer and PM.

4

Design Research Partnership

Short chat about working with research.

5

Engineering Partnership

Short chat about collaborating with engineers.

6

Outro

Final questions and wrap up with the hiring manager.

Finally, when we get to the offer stage we'll ask for 3 references. Ideally one is a past manager.
