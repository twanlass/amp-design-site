Interview Process

We know interviewing for a new design role can be a lot. We want to make it transparent and as stress-free as possible. You'll have a chance to meet a diverse group of people from the team including PMs, Engineers, and Researchers.

Here's what to expect:

Intro

Quick intro chat with the hiring manager and time for questions.

Case Study

Present one or two projects and discuss your work with the team.

Design Jam

Live collaborative design exercise with another Designer and PM.

Research Partnership

Short chat about working with research.

Engineering Partnership

Short chat about collaborating with engineers.

Outro

Final questions and wrap up with the hiring manager.

Offer

We'll ask for 3 references. Ideally one is a past manager.
